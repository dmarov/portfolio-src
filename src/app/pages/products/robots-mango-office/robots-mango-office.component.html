Robots MANGO OFFICE

MANGO OFFICE is a provider for B2B solution in realm of telecommuniactions from Moscow, Russian Federation.

It has large ecosystem of products. One of the products is "Virtual Assistant" service. It provides solution for creating and managing virtual employees also known as "robots".

"Robot" can make outcoming calls through an entire clients base, accept incoming calls, handle chat for all the different platforms such as "Telegram" or "Whatsapp".

Script constructor enables major possibilities for scripting with conditions, cycles, SMS and email notifications, handling user input and lots of other features used to automate routine.

I developed front-end.

  • Angular Material
  • Socket.io
  • Jest
