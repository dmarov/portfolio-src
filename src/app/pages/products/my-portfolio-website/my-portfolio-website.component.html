My Portfolio Website

Dmitry Marov is an awesome software developer from Russian Federation. He decided that it is a good idea to make such website, which will display all the awesome projects he was involved in during his career.

He hopes that this website will provide all the information in which potential employer might be interested in.

  • Stylelint
  • Prettier
