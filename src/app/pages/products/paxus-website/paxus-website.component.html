Paxus Website

Paxus is a company from Archangelsk, Russian Federation. It constructs and sells offroad vehicles, which can be customized depending on customer's needs. The website is an online shop + CMS.

Visit

Catalog includes product constructor where customer can customize vehicle parts such as engine, color, features, etc. Available customization for product can be assigned in CMS.

I developed backend and Implemented most complex parts of frontend logic.
